Caramel Machiato

Description

Slightly sweetened milk on the bottom, ice in the middle, bittersweet espresso on top, and rich caramel sauce all over the cup... Isn't this what you are craving this summer? Get ready to make your very own Iced Caramel Macchiato!

The How and The To!

things you need

  • Vanilla Syrup
  • Milk
  • Espresso
  • Caramel sauce

things you do

  1. Add vanilla syrup to the cup. (2 tsp - 12oz cup, 3 tsp - 16oz cup, 4 tsp - 20oz cup). Of course, you can adjust the sweetness to your liking.
  2. Add milk over the syrup (approx. halfway).
  3. Add ice, reserving room for espresso.
  4. Add espresso (1oz - 12oz cup, 2oz - 16oz cup, 3oz - 20oz cup).
  5. Drizzle with caramel sauce on top and enjoy!
